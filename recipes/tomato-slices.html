Baked Tomato Slices

Home

Description

Sun-ripened tomatoes, thinly sliced and baked to perfection.
Each bite offers a burst of sweet, savory flavor, with a hint of herbs and a tender, yielding texture.
A simple yet elegant side dish or light appetizer.

Ingredients

  • Large ripe tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Dried oregano
  • Garlic powder
  • Black pepper
  • fresh parsley (optional, for garnish)

Recipe

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Wash and slice the tomatoes into 1/4-inch thick rounds.
  3. Arrange the tomato slices in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Drizzle the tomato slices generously with olive oil.
  5. Sprinkle evenly with dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the tomatoes are tender and slightly caramelized at the edges.
  7. Remove from the oven and, if desired, garnish with fresh chopped parsley before serving.
